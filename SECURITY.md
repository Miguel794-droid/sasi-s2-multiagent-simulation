## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in SASI, please report it by emailing:

**miguelsaavedravado440@gmail.com**

We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours and provide a timeline for resolution.

## Response Timeline

- Critical vulnerabilities: 7 days for patch
- High severity: 14 days for patch  
- Medium/Low severity: 30 days for patch

## Scope

This policy covers:
- Manipulation of the E parameter (human effectiveness)
- Bypass of the structural veto mechanism
- Unauthorized access to simulation APIs
- Data integrity issues in the viability calculation

Thank you for helping keep SASI secure!
